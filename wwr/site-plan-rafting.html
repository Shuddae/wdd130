Overview

Purpose

Reach a Wider Audience: WE can be able to reach potential customers who are searching for rafting adventures in our area. so being online ensures we're visible to these potential customers. Customer Engagement and Communication:We are able to respond to customer inquiries, and build a community around our business. Engage with our customers via vedios, photos and many more Booking and Reservations: online platform enables customers to easily check availability, view trip details, and make bookings directly.

Audience

Scenario 1: Question: How does your Rafting company discover and research rafting site Answer: We do our research online for rating sites that read reviews, visit websites to compare trip options, view photos and videos of past trips, Having a clear understanding of our audience makes it easy for our rafting site to attract customers.

Scenario 2: Question: What are the key motivations of your target audience for choosing a rafting trip? Answer: Our target audience is motivated by the desire for adventure, the thrill of navigating rapids, and the opportunity to experience nature up close. We are also attracted to the social aspect of rafting, enjoying shared experiences with friends, family, or other adventure enthusiasts.

Scenario 3: Question: Who is the ideal customer for your water rafting adventures? Answer: Our ideal customer is typically aged between 25 and 45, enjoys outdoor activities, and seeks adrenaline-pumping experiences like whitewater rafting. They are likely to have some prior experience with adventure sports and are looking for professionally guided, safe, and exhilarating rafting trips.
